# Past Continuous Tense - Practice Questions

## Part 1: Multiple Choice (Basic)
Choose the correct option to complete the sentence.

**1. I ________ a book when the phone rang.**
- A) was reading
- B) were reading
- C) read
- D) am reading

**2. They ________ playing football yesterday afternoon.**
- A) wasn't
- B) didn't
- C) weren't
- D) aren't

**3. ________ she sleeping when you knocked on the door?**
- A) Were
- B) Was
- C) Did
- D) Is

**4. We were eating dinner ________ the power went out.**
- A) while
- B) as
- C) when
- D) then

**5. While the teacher was speaking, the students ________ taking notes.**
- A) was
- B) are
- C) were
- D) did
